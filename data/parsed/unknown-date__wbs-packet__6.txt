Fee Changes and Timeline 
This memo outlines which departments are requesting fee changes as well as the timeframe to change 
or establish new fees to be effective May 1, 2026. 
To change fees for County services (except as set by State or Federal law), the following process is 
required per Order 2000-25 adopted March 13, 2000, and approved by the voters on May 16, 2000.  In 
addition, ORS 203.115 only allows fees to be increased no more than once every six months.  
The department requesting fee changes is: 
Community Development - Planning 
The timeline is as follows: 
Tuesday, March 3, 2026  
Packet of proposed fee requests to BCC for review 
Wednesday, March 11, 2026 
BCC adopts first Order setting a public hearing at WBS 
Wednesday, March 18, 2026 
First publication in newspaper and website 
Wednesday, March 25, 2026 
Second publication in newspaper and website 
Wednesday, April 1, 2026 
Public Hearing & Adopt Second Order on Fee Requests 
Attached are the fee change requests and backup documentation. Please note that any delays in the 
timeline may result in fees not being effective until after May 1st. Please let me know if you have any 
questions after your review. I am happy to gather any additional information.

BEFORE THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS FOR JOSEPHINE COUNTY 
STATE OF OREGON 
 
In the Matter of Uniform Procedure for Setting Fees Charged 
) 
 
by County:  Community Development (Planning) – Various Fees ) 
 
Order No. 2026-019 
 
 
  
 
 
 
 
 
WHEREAS, at the May 16, 2000 election, the voters of Josephine County approved County Measure 17.73 (Board 
of County Commissioners Order No. 2000-25 dated March 13, 2000) establishing a uniform procedure for setting 
fees charged by Josephine County in compliance with the Josephine County Charter; and 
WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners set a time and place for a hearing to allow public comment prior to 
adopting fee schedules for certain County programs; and 
WHEREAS, following due and proper notice, the Board of Commissioners, having heard this matter at 9:00 a.m. 
on Wednesday, April 1, 2026 at a public hearing on the proposed fees does make the following FINDINGS: 
(1) The proposed fees, as set out in the Exhibits attached hereto, for the following County programs: Community 
Development (Planning) are consistent with the intent of the measure establishing a uniform procedure for setting 
fees charged by the County; the provision of the services will be borne by the users of the services and not by the 
taxpayers and general fund of Josephine County; and 
(2) The cost of the proposed fees is calculated to cover the cost of providing the services and administration of 
the programs directly related to the proposed fees. 
NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED as follows: 
1) The Board of Commissioners declares its approval of the fee schedules, as set out in the Exhibits attached 
hereto, for the following County programs: 
• 
Community Development (Planning) 
 2) The fees established hereunder shall be effective May 1, 2026.

WHEREAS, Josephine County is the fee owner of certain real properties and 
improvements, acquired by the County by foreclosure of delinquent tax liens, or otherwise, in 
which property is located at 250 E. Hanby Lane, Cave Junction, Josephine County, Oregon, 
described as follows: 
Lot 12, COTTAGE BUSINESS PARK, Josephine County, Oregon, according to the 
Official Plat thereof, recorded in Volume 9, Page 324, Plat records. 
WHEREAS, it has been discovered that the real property is a detention pond for a 
nearby development located in the City of Cave Junction. 
WHEREAS, ORS 271.330(1) authorizes the County to relinquish title to its real property 
to another governmental body to be used to public purposes; and 
WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners has determined that it is in the best interest of 
the public to transfer ownership of the property to the City of Cave Junction, without consideration, 
provided that said properties shall be used and continue to be used, for a public purpose; 
NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the real property described in Exhibit 
A, attached hereto, be conveyed and transferred to the City of Cave Junction, said property to be 
used for a public purpose, pursuant to ORS 271.330(1 ). 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the Board of Commissioners shall execute all documents 
necessary to accomplish the transfer.

Special Meeting of the Josephine County Board of Commissioners:         
March 6, 2026 
11:00 a.m. – Board of Commissioners Conference Room 
Attending: Commissioners Ron Smith, Colene Martin, and Gary Richardson and Kassie Austin as 
Recorder 
The meeting was called to order at 1:30 p.m. 
1. Declaration of Potential Conflicts of Interest 
The board reviewed today’s agenda and determined that there are no conflicts of interest. 
2. Public Employment and Labor Law 
Kyle Abraham, the county’s employment attorney made an introduction as to his background and 
engagement with the county. The board discussed how policies apply to union and non-union 
employees. Mr. Abraham briefed the board on the BOLI complaints and tort claim notices. In depth 
conversation reviewed all four unions that county employees have, and which departments, and 
employees fall under the unions. The timeline for when the bargaining agreements term, and when 
negotiations need to take place was discussed. Public sector issues such as due process, searches and 
drug testing were discussed.  
3. Other: (ORS 192.640(1)…Commissioners may introduce other matters for consideration by the board) 
None head. 
4. Additional Subject Matters from Commissioners/Announcements 
None heard. 
The meeting adjourned at 1:50 p.m.

Pursuant to notice through the media and in conformance with the Public Meeting Law, Ron Smith, Chair, called the 
meeting to order at 9:00 a.m. followed by the Pledge of Allegiance.  
Items discussed were as follows: 
1. CALL MEETING TO ORDER: Pursuant to notice through the media and in conformance with the Public Meeting Law, Ron 
Smith, Chair, called the meeting to order at 9:00 a.m. 
2. DECLARE A POTENTIAL CONFLICT OF INTEREST: No conflicts 
3. ADMINISTRATIVE ACTION(S) IN CONSIDERATION OF: 
The commissioners reserved their right to declare a potential conflict of interest.  
a. Resolution No. 2026-004; Josephine County Supplemental Budget for Fiscal Year 2025-2026 
Ruth Nelson, Finance Director, spoke to the board about the Josephine County Supplemental Budget, increases and 
dates of the committee meeting.  
b. Board Order No. 2026-008; In the Matter of the Sale of County Land Remaining Unsold Following 
Sheriff’s Auction September 17, 2025; Quitclaim Deed for Sale of County Land located at New Hope 
Rd – R324954 
Helene Lulich, Property Manager, spoke to the board about Board Order No. 2026-008 for New Hope Road sale. This 
is a surplus property. This property was listed at counter sale. Offer met minimum requirement and the offer was 
accepted by the Board on March 3, 2026. The balance after admin cost will go to the reserve account.  
Commissioner Richardson asked if neighboring property owners are aware of this property? Ms. Lulich concluded 
that they were.  
4. REQUESTS/COMMENTS FROM CITIZENS: (Each person will be given three (3) minutes to speak) 
Cheryl Bruner Williams, who spoke in favor of the current Board of Commissioners, asked for a meeting 
with Commissioners about Williams Community Forest Project.  
Victor, Grants Pass spoke about Michael Seller and Admin Leave policy, media policy, Timberland agenda 
and Evergreen stop payment. 
Mark Jones, Grants Pass, spoke about Wildfire Season and Emergency Management Department 
Judy Aherns, Josephine County, spoke about Easter and her cupcakes, about Homelessness issues Public 
School system.  
5. BOARD MAY REVIEW PUBLIC COMMENTS/QUESTIONS:  
The Board addressed public comments  
 
Board Discussion and Action – Agenda Item 3(a) 
Commissioner Richardson made a motion to approve Resolution No. 2026-004; Josephine County Supplemental 
Budget for Fiscal Year 2025-2026,. Motion died for lack of a second. 
 
Board Discussion and Action – Agenda Item 3(b) 
Commissioner Richardson made a motion to approve Board Order No. 2026-008; In the Matter of the Sale of County 
Land Remaining Unsold Following Sheriff’s Auction September 17, 2025; Quitclaim Deed for Sale of County Land 
located at New Hope Rd – R324954, seconded by Commissioner Martin. Upon roll call, Commissioner Richardson 
– abstained, Commissioner Martin – yes, Commissioner Smith – yes. Motion carried 3-0.
